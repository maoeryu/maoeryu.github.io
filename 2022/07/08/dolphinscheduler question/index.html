dolphinscheduler question

Posted on 2022-07-08 Edited on 2022-07-26 In 协同框架

dolphinscheduler日常遇到的问题.

资源中心权限问题

无权限创建文件夹.

时区

日志时间少8小时.
修改bin/env/dolphinscheduler_env.sh,将utc修改为GMT+8.

找不到jdbc驱动

将mysql驱动分别复制到以下路径,
alert-server/libs/mysql-connector-java-8.0.22.jar
api-server/libs/mysql-connector-java-8.0.22.jar
master-server/libs/mysql-connector-java-8.0.22.jar
tools/libs/mysql-connector-java-8.0.22.jar
worker-server/libs/mysql-connector-java-8.0.22.jar
